Our Continent Song
(Written by the students with teacher support)

Hello North America
Say goodnight to Asia
How cold is Antarctica?
How hot is South America?

What’s it like in Africa?
I’d love to see Australia
Australia is the best
And Europe is the next!

Sailing on the oceans
With dolphins and the whales
Pacific to the Indian
Maybe we’ll see some sails
br> Atlantic to the Arctic
We can’t believe it’s real
We’re following the long trails
Of the sharks and the seals

We’ve been to all the continents
And now we know our way
Together we will see the world
From the oceans to the bay

Together we will sing and learn
The song of continents
Then we will share it all
With our family and friends

Praise or Encourage

Should I praise or encourage?
How do I know what's what?
Being told I'm really good,
Or what is it I'm good at?

Praise can make my head spin
With wonders at my prowess
But if I am encouraged
I start to understand the process

Of what it is I do that works
Or that which misses the mark
With praise all I ever know
Is if I am a "bright spark"!

When someone stops praising me
I wonder how I failed
To impress my praiser
And dropped so low in the scale


Am I no longer such a "good girl"?
Did I not do "great work!"?
Don't I have any "talent"?
Did I act like a jerk?

Now with encouragement
Here are the things I feel
Acceptance and self-confidence
The words I hear reveal

My effort will pay back in spades
Taking a risk is safe
Now I've learned
to set my goals
Beyond the limits of praise!

Excuses Excuses

I know a little boy
Who always tells me why
He cannot finish his work
Or feels the need to cry

He peppers me with questions
He tells me he is quick
He wants to keep on talking
But I can see through all his tricks!

He uses many words
To tell me what he thinks
When I ask him to write them down
He does not want to, "It stinks!"

I hope to end this debate
And get him working very soon
Maybe I can get him to try
'Cause, "Darn it! It is June!"

Friendship

Friendship is togetherness,
sharing thoughts and hope
It's in times of trouble,
that extra length of rope

Friendship is loyalty,
to every word you spoke
It's remembering promises,
which for others you broke

Friendship is the story,
of souls that reunite
Making memories for the morrow,
sharing love that never dies

Friendship is a mystery,
to those who know not why
A friend will stand-by until the end,
and never walk-on by

You don't come by often,
this kind of friend
But when you do you know,
this friendship will never end.

Ask me not

Ask me not to stay silent
When a friendship is at stake
To live in isolation
Would be a big mistake

Ask me not to forget you
'Cause memories make a life
Though I leave the past behind
I carry on the inner strife

Ask me not to move on
Though one day I know I will
I must walk towards the future
Who can truly stand still?

Ask me to be loyal,
to be faithful and true
Ask me to respect your silence
And let you be you

Ask me to wait in patience
And cause you not such woe
But please my dear,
Ask me not...
To ever let you go!

Flight to Freedom

Volez volez loin
Ce n'est pas votre monde
Ce n'est pa votre vie
It n'ya pas d'amour ici

Et quand nous aurons la liberte
Vous reviendrez chez vous
Et puis nous vivrons
Ensemble en bonheur

Translation

Fly fly far away
This is not your world
This is not your life
There is no love here

And when we get our liberty
You can return home
And we will live
Together in happiness

Times Flies

Voyez comme le temps passe
Ou sont les ans?
Avant j'etais une jeune femme
Maintenant je suis differente

Si je veux rentrer la-bas
Je pense que je ne peux pas
Parce que les jours sont partis
Et je suis vieux

Translation

See how the time passes
Where are the years?
Once I was a young woman
Now I am different

If I wanted to return there
I don't think I can
Because those days have gone
And I am old!
